Cinnamon Toast

Description

This is quite possibly the easiest snack to make with only 4 ingredients!

Ingredient List

  • 1-2 Slices of Bread
  • 1/2 tsp of Cinnamon
  • 1/2 tsp of Sugar Onion Sliced
  • 1 tsp of Unsalted Butter

My Step-by-Step Process

  1. Place your bread in the toaster or oven until slightly crunchy
  2. Spread butter evenly on toast
  3. Sprinkle sugar and cinnamon on buttered toast and voila!Enjoy this delicious treat!
