Brisket Shin Ramen

Description

Are you looking for a way to make "varsity" level instant ramen? Follow these simple steps to level-up your instant ramen!

Ingredient List

  • 2-4 slices of Left-Over Brisket
  • 1 package of Shin Black or Regular Shin Ramen
  • 1/8 Onion Sliced
  • 1 Chive Sliced
  • 1-2 Soft Boiled Egg(s)
  • 1/2 tsp Sesame Oil

My Step-by-Step Process

  1. Bring 2 1/3 cups of water to a boil. Add in seasoning packet(s), sliced onion and chives
  2. Let onion and chive soften (about a minute) then add noodles and sliced brisket
  3. Turn off and remove from heat once the noodles are al dente
  4. Pour into large bowl and plate with soft boiled egg. Enjoy!
